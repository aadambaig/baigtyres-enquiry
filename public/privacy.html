How we use your details

We use what you give us to contact you about your enquiry, to confirm your vehicle, and — only if you tick the offer box or submit the offer pop-up — to send occasional offers.

Your enquiry

Your name, mobile, email, registration and message are sent to our enquiries inbox so we can get back to you about the job. Enquiries are delivered by email and held only briefly in a secure delivery queue so we can guarantee we receive them; they are not published anywhere on this site, and your details are never sold or shared for marketing by third parties.

Vehicle confirmation

When you enter your registration we check it against official UK vehicle data (DVLA/DVSA) to confirm basic details such as make, model, year, fuel and MOT status. We only store the registration you give us alongside your enquiry.

Marketing (only if you opt in)

Joining our list for "10% off your first order" adds your contact to a separate marketing list for occasional offers and new-service announcements. We keep one active record per contact where possible. Unsubscribe any time — every message includes a way to opt out.

Your rights

Email enquiries.baigtyres@gmail.com to see or delete what we hold on you.
