What we supply & fit

  • Maxton Design UK body kits — vehicle-specific kits from one of Europe's biggest styling brands, supplied and fitted as a complete job.
  • Front splitters & lips — sharpen the front end and lower the visual stance.
  • Side skirts — tie the front and rear together for a planted, factory-plus look.
  • Rear diffusers — aggressive rear-end detail that frames the exhaust.
  • Spoilers & wings — from subtle ducktails to full GT-style wings.
  • Full custom kits — mixed-brand or bespoke styling if you want something different.

Finishes: gloss black, carbon-style and painted-to-code options where the manufacturer offers them.

Why Maxton Design?

Maxton Design parts are designed per model, not universal — which means correct mounting points, correct lines and no filler-and-hope fitting. The range covers hundreds of models from hot hatches to German saloons, so there's almost certainly a kit for your car.
