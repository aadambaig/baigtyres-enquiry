What we build

  • Resonator deletes — the first step in sound modification: removing the mid-pipe resonator gives a fuller, slightly louder tone without going drastic.
  • Silencer / muffler (backbox) deletes — the second step, and a bigger jump in volume and presence than a resonator delete: deeper, louder tone and a cleaner rear end.
  • Tailpipes & exhaust tips — single, twin, quad; polished, black or carbon-style finishes, angle-cut or rolled.

Pick your sound level

Level 1
Tips only. Sharper look, standard sound.
Level 2
Resonator delete. The first step — a fuller, slightly louder tone.
Level 3
Silencer / muffler delete. A bigger jump — deeper and noticeably louder than a resonator delete.

Not sure? Tell us the car and what you want it to sound like — we'll recommend the level and tell you honestly what's road-appropriate for your vehicle.

Done properly

Every job is custom measured to your car — we fit the components, seal the joints and run it up to temperature to check for leaks before handover. You get the ideal sound with the ideal service.
